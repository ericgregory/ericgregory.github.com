About

I write about science fiction, technology, imaginary people, and happenings around my hometown of Carrboro, NC.

My stories have appeared or are forthcoming in Lightspeed, Strange Horizons, Shine: An Anthology of Optimistic SF from Solaris Books, Zombies: More Recent Undead from Prime Books, and elsewhere. I earned my MFA from North Carolina State University in Raleigh, NC. I've written non-fiction for The Internet Review of Science Fiction and Fantasy Magazine and served as a guest editor for Bull Spec.
